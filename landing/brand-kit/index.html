Buttons

Three button styles: gradient primary, outline secondary, and pill CTA.

Specs

Primary: background linear-gradient(90deg, #3D95CE, #5DAAE0), color white, weight 600
Outline: border 1px solid rgba(51,65,85,0.8), color #F1F5F9, weight 500

Voice & Tone

Direct, confident, developer-friendly. No hype, no jargon for its own sake. Technical but approachable.

Do

  • Use concrete numbers (5 minutes, 10K blocks)
  • Show code examples and terminal commands
  • Address the reader directly (you, your)
  • Be specific about what Ethernal does
  • Use developer terminology correctly
  • Short sentences, lead with the benefit

Don't

  • Use hype words (revolutionary, game-changing)
  • Overuse emojis (🍷 in footer is the only one)
  • Make claims without proof
  • Use jargon without purpose
  • Sound corporate or stiff
  • Use generic placeholder icons

Headline Patterns

[Benefit] for your [thing] → "Etherscan for your blockchain"
[Action] in [timeframe] → "Deploy in 5 minutes"
Built for [audience] → "Built for Developers"
[Statement]. [Support]. → "Built in the open. MIT licensed."
